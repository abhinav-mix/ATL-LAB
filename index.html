About ATL Lab

Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL) is a workspace where young minds can give shape to their ideas through hands-on do-it-yourself mode and learn innovation skills.

Our project focuses on solving real-world problems using cutting-edge technology, from robotics to IoT, fostering a culture of curiosity and problem-solving.
